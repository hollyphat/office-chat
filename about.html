Aim and Objectives

The study is carried out to fulfill the following aims and objectives

  • To ensure effective communication between student and member staff of the school
  • Users using this application can use it to chat within themselves and others.
  • Efficient relay of information back and forth in real time.
  • Complaint can be lodge on the mobile app to reach to the lecturer or whoever the complaint is meant for
